Vulnerability Management Programs

September 15, 2020

Why VM Program The components of a VM Program Tools

A critical component of keeping your environment safe from exploitation is knowing what vulnerabilities exist, prioritising them for remediation and managing that process. That can look different from org to org based on a variety of factors to numerous go into here. Suffice it say there is no one good solution and teams will have to pick and choose the components of a vulnerability management program that works for them. As with all things, be prepared for a burn in period where the process and system may have to be tweaked to be effective and get the buy-in from stakeholders.

Part 1:

Buy-in from stakeholders Established workflow Education Tracking system

Woah.. where’s the technical stuff? Deploying an effective vulnerability management program is more about the high level cooperation of various teams to discover, prioritise and remediate. The technical nitty gritty comes in when you are scanning for vulnerabilities, verifying them and then remediation. If you try to tackle the technical details first, you’ll have a tougher time getting traction.
